Welcome to the Email Marketing Copy Generator!

This form uses a Large Language Model (LLM) to generate email marketing copy based on your inputs. Currently, you can add your preferred tone to the message and it will generate an answer. Additionally, we plan to add support for additional feature, such as customizing emails by user profile and activity.

How to use this app

  1. Describe the tone your email should have
  2. If similar requests have been made in the past, you will see a few of those results. If one of them works for your needs, click "use this prompt", copy and paste it into your CMS and you're done! We'll ask you to rate how well this response meets your needs.
  3. If none of the existing responses meet your needs, click "generate new response" and you will see a new response based on your input. click "use this prompt", copy and paste it into your CMS and you're done! We'll ask you to rate how well this response meets your needs.
